Frequently asked questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods for your convenience.

Can I change my subscription plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

HIs there a free trial available?

We currently do not offer a free trial, but you can explore our platform with our monthly subscription option before committing to a yearly plan.

Are there any additional fees or hidden charges?

No, there are no hidden fees. The prices listed on our website are all-inclusive, and you'll only be charged the amount specified for your chosen subscription plan.

Do you offer volume discounts for large organizations?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for large organizations with multiple users. Please contact our sales team to discuss your organization's needs and receive a personalized pricing quote.

Can I transfer my subscription to another user or organization?

Subscriptions are non-transferable between users or organizations. Each subscription is tied to an individual user account and cannot be transferred to another user or organization.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you will retain access to our platform until the end of your billing cycle. After that, your access will be suspended until you resubscribe.

Do you offer discounts for students or educators?

Yes, we offer special discounts for educational institutions. Please contact our support team to learn more about our education pricing options.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with my subscription?

We strive for customer satisfaction, but refunds are generally not offered. However, we're committed to resolving any issues you may encounter with our platform, so please reach out to our support team for assistance.

What happens if I exceed the usage limits of my subscription plan?

If you exceed the usage limits of your subscription plan, you may be prompted to upgrade to a higher tier or pay for additional usage. Alternatively, you can adjust your usage to stay within the limits of your current plan.

How do I contact customer support for billing inquiries?

You can reach our customer support team for billing inquiries through our support portal or by emailing billing@hacktherealm.com We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have regarding your subscription.

Do you offer custom invoicing or billing arrangements for corporate clients?

Yes, we offer custom invoicing and billing arrangements for corporate clients. Please contact our sales team to discuss your invoicing requirements and set up a custom billing arrangement.
